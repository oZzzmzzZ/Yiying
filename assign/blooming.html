Blooming Paper

A Portable Floristry Box

Flower arrangement needs a number of fresh cut flowers that are short lived, and dissipative. Paper is able to be rolled, shaped, and glued together to create decorative components. I create a set of portable and personalized components including 3-D miniature flowers, tool to replace fresh cut flowers, as raw materials for flower-arranging play.
